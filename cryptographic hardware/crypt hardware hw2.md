$$
\begin{align}
\implies A(x) \times B(x)  &= x^{2}+x+x+1+x^{3}+1 \\
&=x^{3}+x^{2}
\end{align}
$$

$$\begin{align}
\implies A(x) \times B(x)  &= (x^2+1)(x+1) \\
& =x^{3}+x+x+1 \\
&=x^{3}+ 1
\end{align}$$
